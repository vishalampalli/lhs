EMBLEM & MOTTO

Bharata Rratna Sir MV Educational Society has it's emblem a book of knowledge supported by lotus. symbolizing purity, grace and dignity Books are the means to worldly success but man has to grow beyond the worldly by pursuits to discover the ever ascending mind and the soul of humanity which should radiate by its inner strength and light, symbolized by a flame of light at the centre when we are awakened to the truth it is then that we live only for the Divine All these are con- tained within a circle that symbolizes integration, disci- pline and fullness. Inscribed at the bottom of the circle that contains the "Achieving new heights" which translated sums up the motto

"That which leads us to liberation is education. Liberation means freeing oneself from all forms of bondage. As one sheds these restrictions one becomes independent and perfect through achievement. This achievement is not an ordinary achievement but it should be in new heights that institution which imparts such an education is really a temple

AIMS & OBJECTIVES

Bharata Ratna Sir MV. Educational Society has been founded with the objective of running edu cational institution to tap and nurture local talent foster all round development of the students and create an ideal atmosphere for achieving excellence in the sphere of education, This society is committed wholly to the cause of education and improving edu- cational standards in this area. It is with this genuine concern at heart Bharata Ratna Sir MV Educational Society has opened this "Little Hearts School" Aflated to the Karnataka state syllabus as part of efforts to promote excellence

This society has been the aspiration as well as a long felt need of the people of this area who are so disturbingly shaken by the pace of business and social transformation that parents hardly ever find time to bestow personal attention on their wards This school fulfils this sole objective by taking over the parental responsibilities besides, this school imbibes in students self confidence necessary to meet the challenges of life by building their character on the strong foundations of self discipline, leader- ship co-existence and sacrifice.
